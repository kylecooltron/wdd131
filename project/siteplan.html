Site Purpose

This site is for serious fly fishing enthusiasts who want to keep track of their favorite fishing spots. It's meant to be a private tool for users to log locations, conditions, and catches. Some day it would have the ability to share select information with specific friends, but the main focus is personal record-keeping. There will be pages for catch records, gear tracking, and an interactive map preloaded with some basic information about fly fishing locations in Utah. Users will be able to place their own markers on the map. The record keeping will automatically attach timestamps, seasonal information, and possibly weather conditions. The site will also probably link out to some local fly shops and resources.

Scenarios

  • Where can I keep track of the fish I catch and still keep my favoriate spots secret?
  • How can I remember what I was using that one day I caught a bunch of fish two years ago?
  • How do I mark and optionally share my favorite fishing locations?
